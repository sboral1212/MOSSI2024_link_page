Instructions for presenters

For your convenience, here are some key points to consider for seamless participation:

  1. Screen Sharing: Utilize the upward arrow option located at the bottom middle of the Zoom platform to share your screen during the sessions.

  2. Presentation Guidelines: Kindly share your entire screen during your presentation to mitigate any potential technical difficulties.

  3. Hiding floating window: After sharing your entire screen, you may have a top floating window in ZOOM platform, which you can hide by pressing ctrl+alt+shift+H.

  4. Camera Usage: Please keep your camera turned on throughout the duration of the presentation for a more engaging and interactive experience.

  5. Recording Policy: In an effort to document the valuable content presented, we have decided to record each session. If you have reservations about having your presentation recorded, kindly inform us before commencing your presentation.
